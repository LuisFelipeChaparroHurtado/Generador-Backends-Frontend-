About us

About us

In the dynamic universe of web application development, efficient database management stands as a component vital. Secure and reliable execution of CRUD operations (Creation, Reading, Update and Delete) is essential in contemporary applications. In this context, the Integration of mappers in PostgreSQL databases presented as a key solution to accurately represent logic the complex relational structures within the environment programming. Thus arises the prevailing need to design a innovative system capable of generating efficient code intended for CRUD operations in PostgreSQL. This system, through a exhaustive analysis of the database structure, identifies:

  • Models
  • Entities
  • Relations

The main mission is to automate the implementation of CRUD in applications connected to PostgreSQL databases, reducing manual effort and minimizing errors in these operations.

User manual

User manual

Welcome to the User Manual of our management application of PostgreSQL databases! Our application is designed to offer you a comprehensive and efficient solution to manage your data securely and reliably. With a focus on CRUD operations (Create, Read, Update and Delete), our tool allows you to work intuitively with your PostgreSQL databases.


What can you expect from our application?

  • Simplicity and Efficiency: We simplify the process of database administration, allowing you to perform CRUD operations quickly and efficiently.
  • Smart Mapping: Our application uses mappers to logically represent the complex structures relational relationships of your databases, facilitating the understanding and data manipulation.
  • Advanced Automation:Through an analysis of the database structure, our tool identifies models, entities and relationships, allowing you to automate the implementation of CRUD in your applications connected to PostgreSQL.
  • Error Reduction: Our goal is to minimize errors errors and manual effort in CRUD operations, giving you a database administration experience fluid and uncomplicated.

Open your web browser and go to the URL provided for the tool. Enter your login credentials and click the “Sign In” button.
After logging in, you will be directed to the menu main, where you will find a side header with the following navigation options:

  • Menu: It takes you to the main page of the system.
  • Connection form: Access tools code generation.
  • Full screen: Allows expand the screen to full screen.
  • Profile user: Access your profile and view your full name.
  • Exit: Log out and exit the system.
>
  • After logging in, you will be redirected to a form where you must enter the information connection to your relational database.
  • Complete the required fields, such as address server, username, password, the port and name of the database.
  • Click the “Connect” button to establish the connection with the database.
Once the connection has been successfully established, you will be taken to the project view. Here you will find the project tree and the commands necessary to start your project after downloading it.
Explore the various options available, including viewing code, the ability to copy lines or all of the code, and downloading the code.
After the necessary review of the project, you will have the option to download the entire project in a ZIP file.
  • Once you have downloaded the project, unzip it to your local system.
